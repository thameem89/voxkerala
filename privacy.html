Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. VoxKerala is committed to protecting the personal data of our users.

1. Data Collection

We collect anonymized polling data and device identifiers to ensure unique voting and accurate regional analysis. No personally identifiable information (PII) is stored without explicit consent.

2. Use of Data

Polling data is used for analytical research and broadcast purposes only. It is never sold to third parties.
